About me

After a couple of fantastic years as a school teacher, I took the opportunity to reevaluate my life and career and take the first steps towards career-change. Teaching is an amazing profession and I will always be proud of my time as a teacher. It’s still very much a part of my life and I want to hold on to those skills throughout the rest of my career, whatever that may bring.

In 2021 I graduated in digital strategy at Piteå yrkeskola. This year, 2022, my plan is to graduate as a webdeveloper in e-commarce at Changemaker education Boden, also IT - Tech for women (Frontend-development) at Sundsgården folkhögskola. My goal is to create beautiful and user-friendly websites.
